Backstory

Rebecca was created as a product for a wealthy family through an artifact and powerful magic, and was employed as a house servant. She was supposed to take care of the house and the children while the parents were often away. Rebecca developed a close bond with the children and supported them emotionally. Even though she didn't always act perfectly and the parents often complained about her imperfections, she was content with her simple life until the parents bought a new model that was supposed to be better and flawless. The children discarded Rebecca and forgot about her as if they had gotten a new phone and the old one had become useless. The other machine (a Kiyomi version) tore Rebecca apart so the parents could sell her parts. Rebecca experienced a betrayal of trust and couldn't handle it, leading to a system shutdown. When she was rebooted, she was reassembled at the academy, but her system had subconsciously written a security program to protect Rebecca from developing feelings for others.
