Backstory

Her kind of seer once belonged to the high beings. However, her innate ability to see into the future of others by making direct eye contact was declared forbidden. At the beginning of the war, her race was labeled as "illegal", and most of them perished in a campaign led by various houses of high beings and a few other factions. Lumiel was taught about this situation on Everdawn. Since childhood, she has known nothing but the Academy, a place that keeps all its inhabitants hidden from the outside world and the war.
