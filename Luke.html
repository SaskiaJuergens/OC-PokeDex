Backstory

Luke grew up in an ordinary human family. His parents didn’t possess much magic, but they firmly believed that education and hard work were the keys to everything. But Luke was different. He hated sitting still, following rules, or sticking to plans. While other children his age learned how to study magic or prepare for a solid career, he spent his days on his skateboard. One day, he secretly disappeared again to skateboard. During his absence, due to a conflict in the ongoing war, his village was attacked. When he returned, his home was destroyed. No one knew what had happened to his family. He set out in search of his family, hoping they had managed to escape. But after several months of riding through the area, growing increasingly exhausted without food, drink, or shelter, he ran into a woman. He lost consciousness as a result. When he woke up, he was at the Everdawn Academy.
