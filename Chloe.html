Backstory

She and her brother Ethan lived a middle-class life. However, their parents often argued. Ethan would always retreat with her to their shared room, cover her ears, and say, **"Everything will be okay."** One day, Ethan gave her a pair of headphones so she wouldn’t have to hear the yelling anymore. One evening, during yet another argument between their parents, Ethan signaled Chloe to stay quiet and remain in their room. Then, for the first time, he left her alone while their parents fought. Hours later, he returned—with the police in tow. Ethan stood frozen, staring at the floor, until Chloe took his hand. Only then did he look up at her, forcing a strained smile before telling her that their mother was dead and their father was on the run from the police. Later, their father was found dead, presumed to have taken his own life. The two were supposed to be sent to an orphanage, but before that could happen, the words of a certain headmistress reached the authorities, and the siblings were instead taken in by the academy.
