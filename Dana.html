Backstory

Dana was born as a deer-being, but she hated her lower half. She wanted to walk, dance, and sit like others—not stand on hooves. Her family was a small group of nomadic deer-folk who lived in the forests. There, the old traditions were everything. "Do not transform, accept what you are." But Dana couldn't accept it. She secretly practiced magic to turn her deer legs into human legs, even though, at first, the transformation only lasted for a few seconds. One day, blood magic hunters came. They hunted magical beings for profit, crystals, blood, horns. Her entire family was slaughtered. Dana hid, pressing her hand over her mouth to muffle her sobs. The next day, a group from EverDawn Academy, who had heard of the incident, found her. The headmistress took her in, and Dana finally had the chance to truly learn magic, even though she never stopped hearing the screams of her family.
