Backstory

Kagami never got to know her father because her mother **beheaded him shortly after becoming pregnant. Her mother was mentally unstable, saw things that weren’t there, and often spoke to certain objects as if they were people. As the oldest of her 13 siblings, who all hatched around the same time, Kagami had to take responsibility early on. She started working all diffrent kind of jobs and began learning the art of swordsmanship after someone gifted her a katana, an act of gratitude after she had protected him from people trying to take his life. Later, she killed her own mother upon discovering that she had planned to eat her children. Unfortunately, Kagami found out too late, when she returned from work, seven of her siblings had already been murdered, while four had managed to escape. The two remaining siblings began laughing hysterically, clawing at their own skin, and repeatedly screaming, "Die! Die! Die!" In response, Kagami stabbed them both. They died within seconds, smiling as they passed. She was never able to find her four missing siblings. For months, Kagami survived on the streets, working and doing whatever she could to get by. That was until people of Everdawn Academy found her and offered her a place among them.
